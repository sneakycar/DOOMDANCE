once upon a time there was a boy and 60 thousand aunts and 60 thousand uncles. two of the aunts went for a ride in a car and they crashed into a building. the building broke to bits and the car banged into the radio tower. the radio tower bashed into another store and the radio tower broke to bits. the store went flying into the dump and one of the cars banged into somebody's house. the house went flying into the street and the street went flying into the dump. the bus from the dump flew into another radio tower and the radio tower banged into the window of a house. the window of the house went flying into somebody's face and the person went flying into another window. and another window went flying into a robber's face and the robber fell into the mud head first. and that mud went splattering over someone else and they dashed into the window of their car. the car went dashing into another radio tower, and the radio tower fell onto somebody's face and that person fell on another window. that window fell on somebody's arm and broke his arm. he had to go to the hospital. another car came flying like crazy and it hit the hospital. the hospital went flying like crazy and landed on the road. the whole world was broken.
